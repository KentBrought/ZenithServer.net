How to Join!

  1. Open Minecraft Launcher: Launch the Minecraft Launcher by double-clicking the Minecraft icon on your desktop or by searching for it in your computer's application menu.
  2. Click on "Multiplayer": In the main Minecraft menu, click on the "Multiplayer" button.
  3. Click on "Add Server": In the Multiplayer menu, click on the "Add Server" button.
  4. Enter Server Information: In the "Add Server" menu, enter the following information:
    a) Server Name (optional): Choose a label: Zenith
    b) Server Address: Enter the IP address of the server: zenithserver.net
  5. Save the Information: After entering the server information, click on the "Done" button to save it.
  6. Join Server: In the Multiplayer menu, click on the server you just added to select it, then click on the "Join Server" button to join the server. Note that this may take a few moments depending on your internet connection and the server's performance.
  7. Start Playing! Once connected to the server, we hope to welcome you into our community!
